From Depression to Reiki

A friend recommended Reiki, so went in for an appointment. On reaching my stomach area I had and uncomfortable hard feeling. She told me that it was mostly emotions and to relax. After she spent some time there the feeling disappeared—my depression and anxiety were gone! I thank God for bringing me to a healer.
